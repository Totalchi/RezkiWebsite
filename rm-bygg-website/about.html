About RM Bygg & Montage

We're not a big
anonymous company

RM Bygg & Montage AB is built around a simple belief: every customer deserves personal service, honest communication, and work you're proud to show off.

We're a small team with a big heart. You always have direct contact with us - not a call centre.

We keep the company deliberately hands-on. That means fewer layers, quicker answers and less of the usual contractor runaround. If we think something is outside scope, we say so. If a better option exists, we explain why.

Most clients come to us for a mix of practical trades: solar, EV charging, roofing, windows and facade work. The common thread is the same on every job: clear planning, tidy execution and a written scope before work starts.
