Before You Decide

Questions worth asking any contractor

  • Will I get a written scope before work starts?
    Yes. The quote should state what is included, what is excluded and what assumptions the price is based on. If conditions on site change, that should be discussed before extra work is approved.
  • What happens if something needs correcting after handover?
    We work with written workmanship coverage. If something is clearly part of our scope and needs correcting, the expectation should be simple: you call, we review it and we sort it out.
  • Do you coordinate specialists if a project needs them?
    Yes. Some jobs need scaffolding, network-operator coordination or other specialist input. In those cases the client should still have one clear main contact and one written scope tying the work together.
  • How quickly do you respond to enquiries?
    We aim to respond within 24 hours on weekdays. For urgent issues, such as active leaks, tell us that directly so we can prioritise the first reply correctly.
